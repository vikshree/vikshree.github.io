Welcome! I'm a robotics researcher working on the topic of robot perception. I hold a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Artificial Intelligence from [Cornell University](https://www.cornell.edu/) where I was advised by [Prof. Mark Campbell](https://campbell.mae.cornell.edu/).

At [SilC](https://silc.com), I am focused on perception tasks for industrial and warehouse applications of FMCW LiDAR. I have developed algorithms for extrisic calibration of LiDAR, facilitating key tasks such as dimensioning and defect detection. 

Before discovering sunshine in Southern California, I was shoveling through Boston winters at [NODAR](https://www.nodarsensor.com/), leading perception efforts for long-range autonomy using stereo-cameras. I was the lead inventor of NODAR's object detection technology, [GridDetect](https://www.nodarsensor.com/products/griddetect).
